How to play

Your main goal is to get a large population of happy users downloading tons of great warez.

People arrive in the room by you inviting them, or by being invited by other operators or superusers.

People stay in the room while they're happy. If they become unhappy they're more likely to leave. Often, having trolls in the room will make people want to leave. Users also do not like being muted, but if they're muted they can still contribute to total download score.

Only a voiced user can post downloads. Users always start out as voiced. Make sure to keep an eye on new users and mute them if you think they'll be a problem! Hopefully your operators will help!

Users who are rivals are less likely to download each others files. Additionally, users can download viruses.

Viruses can cause you do have all sorts of problems from giving out free ops, to kicking enemies or inviting mysterious users. As far as we're aware, there's no way to stop the viruses from taking affect!
